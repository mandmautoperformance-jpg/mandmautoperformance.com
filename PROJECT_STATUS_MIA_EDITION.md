## 💡 THE PSYCHOLOGICAL MOATS NOW IN PLACE

### Data Lock-In
- Users upload their driver's license to the Vault
- Not re-uploading at Sixt (friction, embarrassment)
- Supabase stores encrypted documents
- Users become sticky after first booking

### Telematics Score Lock-In
- Users see their score improve each rental
- Next tier car always tantalizing (~3-5 points away)
- Won't restart at competitors (back to Bronze tier)
- Creates **self-reinforcing loyalty loop**

### Emotional Attachment (MIA Personality)
- MIA has a name, color, voice
- "Talk to MIA" becomes part of user vocabulary
- Brand advocacy ("My concierge is MIA from M&M")
- Harder to replicate than functional features

### Community Engagement (Wall of Fame)
- Photos from rentals become user-generated content
- Public gallery creates social proof
- "Likes" on photos drive engagement
- Users compete for best photo (mild gamification)

### Switching Cost (Multiple Small Frictions)
- Restart score at new company
- Re-upload documents
- Learn new chatbot personality
- Lose M&M Credits
- Leave photo gallery
- **Sum of small frictions = impossible to leave**
