## 🎨 COLOR & LIGHTING SPECIFICATIONS

### Lighting Setup (All Images)
- **Key Light**: 45° angle, slightly elevated, creates main form definition
- **Fill Light**: Opposite side at lower intensity, reveals shadows without losing drama
- **Rim Light** (subtle): Creates separation from background, 2-3x intensity of fill
- **Accent Light** (Turquoise): Directional turquoise/cyan color gel on wheels, undercarriage, select details
- **Background Light** (if applicable): Separate light on background to maintain 90%+ brightness

### Color Grading Principles
1. **Primary Tone**: Cool shadows (blue color cast), warm highlights (golden/turquoise)
2. **Turquoise Accents**: Technology/future-forward feel via LED/neon turquoise on wheels & undercarriage
3. **Contrast**: High contrast between vehicle and neutral backgrounds (minimum 40% brightness difference)
4. **Saturation**: Selective saturation (vehicle vivid, background neutral)
5. **Clarity**: Crisp, clean details on chrome, badges, interior materials

### Background Specifications
- **Hero Images**: Dark/moody atmospheric backgrounds (Chilterns, London, estates) with depth
- **Fleet Cards**: Neutral white/very light grey (90%+ brightness), minimal texture
- **Depth**: Backgrounds slightly soft-focused to emphasize vehicle sharpness
