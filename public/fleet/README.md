# Fleet Photos

Drop real photos of your vehicles here, then map them in
`components/VehicleImage.tsx` (the FLEET_PHOTOS object).

Example:
  1. Save photo as: public/fleet/lambo-huracan.jpg
  2. In components/VehicleImage.tsx add:
       'lambo-huracan': '/fleet/lambo-huracan.jpg',

Recommended: landscape, at least 1200x800px, JPG or WEBP, under ~500KB each.

Vehicle IDs:
  lambo-huracan, ferrari-f8, porsche-911, bentley-continental,
  rolls-ghost, aston-db12, lambo-revuelto, tesla-plaid,
  range-rover-sport, mercedes-amg
